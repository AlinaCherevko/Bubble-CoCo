Bubble Shooter

Dive into "Bubble Coco" and experience thrilling puzzle-solving adventures with Coco and her chicks! Start playing now for a heartwarming journey filled with challenging bubble-shooting puzzles!

Advantages

  • Colorful and engaging graphics

  • Hundreds of challenging levels

  • Fun and interactive game character

  • Regular updates with new features

  • Easy to learn, but hard to master gameplay

  • Great for all ages

About the Game

Join Coco the mother hen and her chicks in "Bubble Coco," a vibrant bubble shooting game. Help them rescue their precious chicks from the cunning fox by solving a variety of colorful and engaging puzzles. Enjoy hundreds of levels, each offering unique challenges and fun surprises.

How to Play

  • Aim and shoot bubbles to match colors

  • Clear levels by freeing all chicks

  • Use special bubbles for explosive effects

  • Plan moves to solve puzzles efficiently.
