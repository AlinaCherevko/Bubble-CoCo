FAQ

  • How do I start playing?

    Download the game from the Google Play Store and hit 'Play'.

  • Can I play without internet?

    Yes, you can play offline but some features require an internet connection.

  • Are there in-app purchases?

    Yes, but they are optional.

  • How can I get more lives?

    Lives refill over time or can be purchased.

  • What are special bubbles?

    These bubbles have unique effects, like clearing entire lines or exploding all bubbles of the same color.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, you can connect with friends for more fun and challenges.

  • Is there a way to reset the game?

    Yes, you can restart any level at any time.

  • What do I do if the game crashes?

    Try restarting your device or reinstalling the game.

  • How often are new levels added?

    New levels are added regularly.

  • Is there a player support service?

    Yes, you can contact support through the game's settings menu for any issues.
